Learn by example.

Get Origami sample files showing how to build common UI patterns and interactions from popular apps.

Paged Scrolling

Shows how to use the Scroll patch to make a layer scrollable and snap to pages.

Collapsible Navigation Bar

Shows how to make a layer freely scrollable and synchronize effects with the scroll position.

Swipe Navigation

Slide in a new layer from the right when a hit area is tapped and go back by swiping.

Compose Modal

Slide in a new window when you tap a button and hook up buttons in that window to dismiss it.

Master Detail

Shows how to change the view on the right side based on taps on the left. Uses an Index Switch to remember the tab.

Photo Zoom

Use a Switch to move between scaled to fit and scaled to fill states.

Slinker

Shows how to chain Pop Animation patches together to get a rope effect.
