See how it’s done.

Become an Origami pro by watching videos that take you step-by-step through creating prototypes.

  • Get Prototype

    Introduction to Origami

    This video will tell you all you need to know to get started building Origami prototypes. By the end of this tutorial we’ll have built an interaction where you can tap to zoom a photo in and out.

  • Get Prototype

    Collapsible Navigation Bar

    We’ll recreate an interaction in Rooms where scrolling the feed will collapse and expand the navigation bar while animating the elements in the bar. You’ll learn how to make a layer scrollable and animate elements based on its scroll position.

  • Get Prototype

    Swipe-away Navigation

    We’ll recreate an interaction in the Rooms app where you can tap on a feed item to slide the post in from the right and swipe the post away with your finger to return back to the feed. You’ll learn how to use the Swipe patch to make a layer swipe-able and how to use the Hit Area patch to take taps on a certain part of the screen.
